Mustafa K. Isik

I'm a software engineer interested in programming languages, virtual machines, algorithms & data structures. I like to collaborate with other developers. I enjoy giving talks on topics near and dear to my heart; videos and slides are available online.

You can follow my status updates and read articles on my blog. From time to time, I indulge myself in a flurry of mundane posts.

I'm an electronic gaming geek. I write for print mags and work on books. In early 2009 I devised and co-hosted SuperHyperTurbo, a video podcast on gaming which became the most successful of its kind in Germany during its one year production run.

I can't sing ... well - but I <3 Karaoke.
